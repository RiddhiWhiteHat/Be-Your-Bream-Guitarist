1. Tuning a Guitar

Here, You will learn how to tune your Guitar So when you will play it, it sounds good.

There are mainly 2 ways to tune a guitar.

1st, To Tune With the help of an app

If you wish to tune with the help of an App, Then I recommend these Apps

  1. GuitarTuna
  2. Martin Tuner, and
  3. insTuner
  4. Guitar Tools

Remember These only works online!

Whereas, the 2nd way is to Tune with the help of your hearing skills, it is also called Ear tuning,

Listen to the sound made by the Tuner, and make your strings sound like that, by moving the

tuning pegs.

(Plus Point! if you use ear tuning it also increases you listening skills!)

The Tuner above is an example of an ear tuner

You hear the sound and then tune your guitar, but if your a beginner

I would higly recommend to use tuning apps.
